## **Research Summary**
### Domain Research

##### Features
- Return to player percentage, average of how much expect to get back when play
  
- 2 different machines, 
  - one high risk, occasionally payout, 
  - one low risk, more frequent payout
  
- Mathematical edge to be profitable 
  - (4% margin, spend 100 earn back 96)

##### General Mechanics
- RNG for slots, separate from past pulls.

##### Special Mechanics
- Special mini game mode that excites player, known as the bonus buys, better odds to win large money or jackpot, creates excitement (Should be primary focus)
  - People play just for the bonus buy or pay or spin just to get the bonus buy faster
  - aesthetics same, payout differs, is higher

- Game speed and betting frequency (faster game speed, higher getting frequency)

- Win animations wouldhave a lot of coins energy and color
- Spin button animated and addictive
- Referall and prizes to hook people in

##### Psychological Mechanism
- Unpredictable rewards payout 
  - to surprise users when they dont expect it

- Illusion of control through interactivity 
    - ex) stop reel, pick bonus outcomes

- Cognitive bias and illusion of patterns
- Near miss effect (win but not quite), makes them want to play more
  
- Losses disguised as wins with lights and noises
  - keeps players engaged, trick people to think they're doing well

- Trading real money for tokens to make them feel like they arent spending actual money
  - reduces psychological friction, make people spend more


##### User Interface
###### General Interface Design
- Big Jackpot numbers visible at all times
###### Themes
- Fruit / Classic — simple, nostalgic, fast gameplay

- Asian Fortune / Dragon (e.g. Jinse Dao Dragon) — red and gold palette, dragon mascot, ornate reel frames, payout numbers displayed on symbols

- Fantasy / Holiday (e.g. Jingle White) — character mascot (Snow White), fruit symbols with glow effects, GRAND jackpot overlay, multiple progressive jackpot tiers visible in header

###### Classic Symbols
- Coins, gold bar, gift box, clover, heart, gold coin, bomb, money bag, playing cards (hearts/spades), diamond, trophy, watermelon, grapes, cherries, orange, pineapple, apple, BAR, dice, star, lucky 7, poker chip
