## Focus Area : Anticipation & Reel Stopping Mechanics

- A major thing ive noticed in slot machines is the anticipation which is created by how the reels spin and stop one by one rather than instantly showing the result.

- Instead of having all reels stopping at the same time each reel stops slowly one by one  with a slight delay building suspense

- Sound design also plays a huge role in this process in this case each reel stop is paired with a clicking or ticking sound and it increases tension and making the player focus on each individual result.

- Near-win situations like two matching symbols and the last reel still spinning create excitement even if the player ultimately loses

- This anticipation mechanic is what keeps users  invested as the delay and buildup make wins feel more rewarding and losses less immediate.

- For our project, implementing staggered reel stopping, synchronized sound effects, and slight delays will be critical to making the game feel engaging and not boring
