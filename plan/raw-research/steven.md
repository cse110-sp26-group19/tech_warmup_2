## Slot Design Choices
- The spin button, reel timing, and win animations are the core design elements that control attention and excitement.
- Staggered reel stopping and near-miss visuals build suspense more effectively than instant full-stop results.
- A clean layout keeps focus on spinning while secondary info (paytable, settings, stats) stays accessible but unobtrusive.

## Distribution of Wins
- Slot outcomes are not uniform; symbol weights are tuned so small wins happen more often than big wins.
- RTP describes long-run return, while volatility describes how that return is distributed across frequent small vs rare large payouts.
- Two games can share similar RTP but feel very different depending on win frequency and jackpot rarity.

## Why This Matters for Our Project
- We should design visuals and probabilities together, not separately, so the experience feels consistent and intentional.
- A simple target is to pick one volatility style (steady vs swingy), then tune reels and feedback around that style.
