## Focus Area : How to make Slot Machines Addictive
- Create reels that stop just short of a jack pot. This makes people feel like they are close to winning which encourages them to keep playing.
- Having each spin take only a short amount of time allows for people to spin more rapidly and think less rationally.
- Using flashy fun animations, bright colorfl lights, and using sounds that engage your brain make people more excited and simulated.
- Having features like allowing people to stop the reel early makes people feel like they have better control over the results.
- Add a animation that adds anticipation before a win adds even more hype and excitement when people finally get a payout, which makes them more likely to continue spinning.
