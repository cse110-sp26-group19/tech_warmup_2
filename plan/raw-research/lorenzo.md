## What Makes Slots Fun
- Random wins keep it exciting
- Big flashing animations when you win
- Reels stopping one by one builds suspense
- Sound effects for spins and wins

## Features to Add
- Daily bonus for logging in
- Win/loss streak counter
- Balance always visible
- Jackpot number big and on screen

## Themes That Work
- Classic fruit (cherries, 7s, bars)
- Casino night (neon lights, city background)
- Dragons / Asian fortune (red and gold)

## What Our Last Slot Machine Was Missing
- No sound
- All reels stopped at once
- No win animation
- No balance tracker

## User Types
- Casual players want quick fun
- Serious players want stats and big wins
