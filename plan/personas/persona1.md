# Persona: Competitive Player

## Name
eugene krabs

## Age
40

## Background
wealthy player who enjoys games with measurable outcomes and tracking performance even in luck based games like this

## Goals
- Track wins, losses, and streaks
- Hit big wins or jackpots
- Feel a sense of momentum while playing
- Enjoy my time while playing

## Motivations
- Seeing streaks (win/loss patterns)
- Chasing “big win” moments
- Feeling like they are on a lucky run
- Comparing past sessions (did I do better this time?)
- Free bonuses here and there make player feel liked

## Frustrations
- No stats or tracking system
- Instant results with no buildup or excitement
- Lack of feedback after each spin
- Feeling like nothing they do matters
- No animations on the screen

## Behavior
- Plays multiple rounds in a row
- Pays attention to streaks (even if random)
- Gets more engaged during near wins or big moments
- Keeps playing longer when they feel “on a roll”

## Needs for Our App
- Stats dashboard (wins, losses, streaks)
- Clear visual/audio feedback for each spin
- Big win animations and near-win effects
- Systems that highlight streaks or patterns

## Key Insight
This user values the feeling of momentum and tracking outcomes, so the app should emphasize streaks, feedback, and exciting win moments.
